Licenses

All licenses require a registered company to be valid and functional.

Tourism and Recreation

1. Tourism License – 25,000$

  • Allows you to operate a travel agency and organize tours.
  • Create tourist attractions and theme parks.
  • Sell tickets for tourist events and transportation services.

Restrictions:

  • Does not include building hotels – separate license required.

2. Hospitality License – 40,000$

  • Allows you to run hotels, hostels, guesthouses, and resorts.
  • Provide lodging services and organize tourist events.

Restrictions:

  • Does not cover restaurant services – requires a separate Restaurant License.

Ecology and Environment

3. Environmental Protection License – 50,000$

  • Manage natural resources and environmental protection efforts.
  • Engage in recycling and renewable energy activities.
  • Build and operate wind and solar farms.

Restrictions:

  • Does not cover mining – separate Mining License required.

4. Organic Farming License – 30,000$

  • Grow crops in an ecological manner, without synthetic fertilizers or pesticides.
  • Produce organic food products.

Restrictions:

  • Does not include animal farming – requires a separate Agricultural License.

Education and Training

5. Educational License – 40,000$

  • Operate schools, universities, and vocational courses.
  • Offer training in various fields like mechanics, programming, and construction.
  • Organize workshops and educational events.

Restrictions:

  • Does not include teaching illegal technologies – requires appropriate licenses.

6. Training License – 20,000$

  • Offer professional and technical training.
  • Train other players in specialized skills.

Restrictions:

  • Does not cover full academic courses – requires an Educational License.

Entertainment and Media

7. Film License – 50,000$

  • Produce films, TV series, and video content.
  • Operate film studios and collaborate with other creators.
  • Distribute films and organize premieres.

Restrictions:

  • Does not include video game production – requires a separate license.

8. Video Game License – 45,000$

  • Create, sell, and distribute video games.
  • Run development studios and collaborate with other game creators.

Restrictions:

  • Does not include film production – requires a Film License.

9. Music License – 35,000$

  • Produce music, organize concerts, and sell tracks.
  • Operate recording studios and collaborate with artists.

Restrictions:

  • Does not include film or game production – requires a Film License or Video Game License.

Trade and Services

10. Retail License – 25,000$

  • Run retail stores and engage in retail sales.
  • Open shopping networks and online stores.

Restrictions:

  • Does not cover the sale of restricted goods like weapons or drugs.

11. Wholesale License – 35,000$

  • Sell goods in bulk to retail stores and other businesses.
  • Distribute products on a large scale.

Restrictions:

  • Does not include retail sales – requires a Retail License.
